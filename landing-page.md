## How it works (3 steps, with icons)

**1. Paste any property link.**
Magicbricks, 99acres, Housing.com, NoBroker — we read the listing, you don't have to copy anything.

**2. We check it against 8 things.**
Listing age. Price vs locality average. Duplicate count across portals. RERA registration. Image reverse-search. Builder complaint history. Owner-name match. Suspicious patterns.

**3. You get a Trust Score in 30 seconds.**
0–100 score. Clear red flags. A checklist of what to verify before paying anyone.

## FAQ

**Is this really free?**
Yes. Forever, for buyers and renters. We charge banks and lenders, not you.

**Does it work for rentals too?**
Yes — sale and rental, residential and commercial.

**Which cities are covered?**
At launch: Bangalore. Adding Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Hyderabad in the next 90 days. RERA + locality data takes time to do properly. We'd rather be accurate in 5 cities than wrong in 50.

**What if the listing is fine?**
You'll see a green Trust Score and a clean checklist. Then go meet the owner with confidence.

**What about title verification?**
We check the listing. For full title due-diligence (sale deed, encumbrance certificate, ownership chain) we partner with Landeed, the title-records specialists. One click from your report.

**You're storing my data?**
We store the listing URL and the report. We don't store your phone, name, or any personal info unless you sign up for Pro alerts.

**How do you handle false flags?**
Every score comes with a confidence level and the exact reasons. If we get one wrong, you can flag it and we'll review within 48 hours.
